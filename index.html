Philosophy

Vision, Mission &
Commerce Thinking

How I think about product, commerce, and the impact that great digital experiences can have at scale.

My Vision

Build digital products that customers trust and businesses rely on — products that simplify complexity, scale globally, and deliver measurable commercial impact.

My Mission

Help large enterprises turn strategy into execution — owning end-to-end customer journeys, using data to guide decisions, and leading cross-functional teams through complex delivery with clarity and pace.

01

Data Over Opinion

Every product decision deserves evidence. I use ContentSquare, SQL, GA, and user research to build business cases from facts — not assumptions. When data contradicts the loudest voice in the room, data wins.

02

Conversion is Confidence

The best conversion optimisation isn't a CTA button colour — it's removing the friction that stops a customer trusting you. Clarity, confidence, and certainty beat persuasion every time.

03

AI Must Be Grounded

AI in commerce is only as good as the data it's grounded in. RAG architectures, domain guardrails, and role-based access aren't nice-to-haves — they're what separates a production AI system from a liability.

04

Headless is a Strategy, Not a Trend

Moving to headless means nothing unless team culture, agile model, and product thinking move with it. Platform modernisation and product maturity must scale together.

05

Commerce at Scale is an Operations Problem

The real complexity in enterprise eCommerce isn't the storefront — it's orchestrating 50 systems, 40 markets, 90 stakeholders, and a live trading platform simultaneously without dropping anything.

06

Agentic Commerce is Now

Autonomous AI agents making real-time merchandising decisions, optimising the digital shelf continuously — this isn't the future. I've shipped it in production.
